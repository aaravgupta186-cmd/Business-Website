Business Planning
Costing

Costing helps a business calculate how much it spends to produce or run its operations. It includes material costs, labour, equipment, and transportation. By knowing the exact total cost, a business avoids losses and makes smart financial decisions. Costing ensures products are priced correctly and helps track where money is being used.

Pricing

Pricing determines how much a business charges for its products or services. It is based on costs, customer demand, and what competitors charge. Good pricing ensures the business earns profit while staying affordable for customers. Strategies like value-based or competitive pricing help businesses attract and retain buyers.

Management

Management involves planning, organising, and controlling business activities. It ensures the team works efficiently and resources are used properly. Good management sets goals, checks progress, and solves problems quickly. Strong leadership helps the business stay consistent, productive, and adaptable.
